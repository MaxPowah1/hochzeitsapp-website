Why WeddingApp?

All the essentials for your big day—handy, organized, and at everyone’s fingertips.

Wedding Day Schedule

Share a minute-by-minute itinerary so guests never miss a moment—from ceremony to cake cutting and beyond.

All Your Locations

List every venue with GPS coordinates—and tap for turn-by-turn navigation to each ceremony, reception, or hotel.

Key Contacts

Store phone and email details for the bride & groom, wedding party, and emergency numbers—one tap to call or message.

Our Story

Craft a heartwarming narrative of how you met, fell in love, and decided to say “I do”—your very own digital love story.

Gift Registry

Create a personalized wishlist so guests know exactly which gifts you’d love to receive and where to find them.

Wedding Menu

Upload your menu card—appetizers through desserts—so everyone can plan their appetite and dietary needs in advance.

Photo Gallery

Let guests upload and browse highlight snapshots in a shared gallery—so you capture every emotion and smile.

Push Notifications

Send real-time alerts for any last-minute updates—schedule changes, announcements, or simple reminders.

Custom Themes

Choose fonts, colors, and background images that match your wedding palette—make the app uniquely yours.

About Us

The idea for WeddingApp arose from our own experiences as wedding guests. We often noticed the lack of a practical solution to make all essential information for the day easily accessible.

We are a small team of friends passionate about technology, design, and weddings. With heart and creativity, we turn your vision into an app that makes your wedding day even more unforgettable for you and your guests.

It is important for us to create each app uniquely, capturing the personal style of the couple. We look forward to supporting you on your big day!
